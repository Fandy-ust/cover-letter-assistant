# My Info

The central truth of your professional background.

## How you use it
You can edit `personal_profile.md` directly to refine your experiences, fix typos, or add specific STAR-style bullet points. 

## How the agents use it
The **Profile Builder** creates and updates `personal_profile.md` by analyzing the raw materials you place in `raw_materials/personal_materials/`. The **Application Advisor** reads this file to match your background against job requirements and formulate a strategy.
